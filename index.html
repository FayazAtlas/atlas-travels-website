Common Questions

Everything you need to know

🏆What makes Atlas Travels the best for holiday bookings?▾
Atlas Travels combines the convenience of online booking with the expertise of a dedicated travel agent. Direct hotel relationships mean exclusive rates you won't find elsewhere. Plus, we're fully ATOL and ABTA protected.
❌What is the zero deposit option?▾
Our zero deposit scheme lets you confirm your hotel booking without paying anything upfront. You pay closer to departure — your holiday is secured without immediate financial pressure.
💎Can I book both luxury and budget hotels?▾
Absolutely. We cater for all budgets — from stylish boutique hotels to ultra-luxury overwater villas and five-star resorts. Whatever your budget, we'll find the perfect match.
✨Does Atlas Travels provide customised holidays?▾
Yes — tailor-made holidays are our speciality. We build every holiday around your exact requirements, from room type and board basis to transfers and excursions.
💰Is there a price match guarantee?▾
Yes! If you find the same hotel for a lower price online or in-store, we'll match it. Simply show us the comparable quote and we'll ensure you get the best possible deal.
🛡️Are my payments protected?▾
100%. Atlas Travels is ATOL bonded (No. 12458) and ABTA member (P7834). In the unlikely event of any issue, every penny you pay is completely safe.
🔄What is the cancellation policy?▾
Cancellation policies vary by hotel. Many of our hotels offer free cancellation up to a certain date. Always check your booking confirmation, and our team is available to help 7 days a week.
